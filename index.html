The Atlas of Furniture Design

The Atlas of Furniture Design is an unparalleled resource, documenting over 1,000 iconic pieces that have shaped the way we live and work. Created by the Vitra Design Museum, it offers an in-depth look into the social, cultural, and technological innovations behind the world's most famous furniture.
