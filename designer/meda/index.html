Alberto Meda

(1945, Italy)

pick random designer

Bio

Prior to 1969
Tudied mechanical engineering at Milan Polytechnic
1973
Technical Director at Kartell
starting in 1979
consultant engineer and designer at Alias, Brevetti, Kartell, CSI, Colomo Design, and Swatch Italia
1981–85
Project director of Alfa Romeo
1983–87
Professor of production technology at the Domuc Academy, Milan
1989
Received Compasso d´oro prize for the “Lola” lamp made of carbon fibers
1990
Solo exhibition in Tokyo
1994
Professor at the Domus Academy; “European Design Prize” and “Compasso d’oro” for his work with Luceplan
since 1995
Lecturer at the Domus Academy
1995
Participates in the “Mutant materials in contemporary design” exhibition at the Museum of Modern Art, New York
2001
Participates in the “Italia e Giappone design come stile di vita” exhibition, Yokohama, Kobe
2002
“Red Dot Award” for Iittala knives
since 2003
lecturer at the IUAV, Venice
2008
“Compasso d'oro” for the LED lamp MIX, Luceplan
